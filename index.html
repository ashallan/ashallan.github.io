Geographic Information Systems Application Specialist

Ashley Allan

I'm a recent Graduate based in Thunder Bay, Ontario.

My passion lies within nature. Many hobbies and interests that I have are based in the wilderness of the Great White North. There is nothing that I find more fulfilling than planning my next excursion.

Born and raised in the Greater Toronto Area, my experience and knowledge of nature was limited to the suburban lifestyle I lived. The excitement and curiosity that I developed for nature and science remained to exist only in theory, satisfied by the books and documentaries I would immerse myself in. It wasn't until I relocated to Northwestern Ontario that I had the opportunities and resources to finally explore my passions through experiences and education.

Education

My academic career began at Confederation College in Thunder Bay, Ontario, where I received my Diploma in Forest and Ecosystems Management. During the time I spent studying at Confederation College, I had the co-operative opportunity to work with both the "Ministry of Natural Resources and Forestry" and a smaller Forestry company "Greenmanlte Forest Inc". The positive experiences and practical skills acquired from these placements were key in the solidification of my desire to pursue a career in Forestry.

After graduating from Confederation College, I decided that the right choice on my career path was to specialize in an area of study that I was not only fascinated by, but challenged by as well. This lead me to the Geographic Information Systems Application Specialist program at Fleming College in Lindsay, Ontario, where I received my Postgraduate Certificate.
